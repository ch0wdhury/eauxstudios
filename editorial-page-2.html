Editorial for @facticemagazine titled 'Statue Maison' May 2020
Her name is Gwen. Gwen has been spending a lot of time at home, sleeping, eating, creating and playing with her outfits. She feels beautiful and empowered when she wears clothes that compliment her body, fits into the moment and represents her current mood. Being able to spend time at home 24/7 has been a blessing and a curse at the same time. Nobody knows what the future brings but she doesn't like to think too much ahead. Turning into a real life statue was a response to Covid-19 and stillness of the moment during its difficult times.
Thank you very much to the great team for creating 'Statue Maison' with us !
